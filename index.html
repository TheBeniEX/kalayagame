The Game

A goddess, a lost past and worlds shaped by ancient forces.

Kalaya – Tales of Fallen Gods is an action-adventure game blending hack n' slash combat, RPG progression, elemental systems, puzzles, alchemy and time manipulation.

Players follow Kalaya, a forgotten goddess living as a human, through mythological worlds shaped by fallen gods, silent colossal creatures and fragments of a larger divine truth.

Gameplay Pillars

Combat, craft and mystery moving as one.

01

Elemental Combat

Switch elements to open enemy weaknesses, shape combos and respond to shifting encounters.

02

Melee & Ranged Action

Move between close-range pressure, ranged tools and stylish action built for readable impact.

03

Time Stop

Freeze moments to solve puzzles, control space and turn impossible combat windows into opportunity.

World / Lore

Realtha was born around the crater of a fallen star.

Its magic shaped the land, its people and the mysteries surrounding the giant silent creatures that appeared across the world. They are immense, peaceful and impossible to understand.

Before the star fell, the creatures began appearing across the planet. Above Realtha stands a colossal tree, provisionally called Yggdrasil, watching over a village touched by celestial power.

In Kalaya, myths are not separate stories. They are fragments of a larger divine truth, connected by fallen gods and ancient forces older than memory.

Built for action, discovery and mythic scale.
